Built breast cancer prediction model using Logistic Regression based on a dataset containing tumor cell characteristics. The model was trained to classify tumors as Benign or Malignant, and then evaluated using Confusion matrix, Cross-validation, and ROC curve. The results show the accuracy obtained, the distribution of correct and incorrect predictions, and the ability of the model to distinguish the classes with the AUC-ROC score, which gives an idea of the effectiveness of the model in breast cancer detection.
